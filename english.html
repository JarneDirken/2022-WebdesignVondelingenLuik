Welcome to the VL

Welcome to the VL


Foundlings are not a new, but fortunately a very rare phenomenon.

Sometimes newborns are left in an unsafe place
so that they rarely survive.

The foundling hatch is there to give a distraught mother the opportunity
to give her child anonymously a warm and safe place.

Following the example of many other countries, in 2000 the VZW Mothers for Mothers
established the first foundlings section in Belgium.
It is located in the Helmstraat 93, 2140 Borgerhout, Antwerpen

The foundling hatch has its own phone number that is 24/7 reachable:
0478 / 81 31 28
This way the mom can contact us anonymously for a chat or for helpful advice and assistance.

About the foundling hatch

The foundling hatch is easily accessible by public transportation. From the main train station you have to walk about 15 minutes.
You can also use a streetcar or bus, which depart from the Koningin Astridplein. The streetcar and bus stop where you must disembark is called "Drink".

  • Bus lines 23, 4xx
  • Streetcar line 24
